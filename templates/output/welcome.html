Thank you for your purchase of the insideFPV FPV drone kit. We're excited to help you get started in this amazing industry.
Here are a few tips to help you get the most out of your new drone:
Practice on a simulator first. This is a great way to learn the basics of flying an FPV drone without having to worry about crashing a real one. We recommend using the Liftoff simulator, which is available on Steam .
Watch our Consultation Video. We've prepared a comprehensive consultation video that covers essential FPV concepts, techniques, and tips. This video will give you a solid foundation to build upon as you progress in your FPV journey.
Please take the time to watch it here.
Start flying in real time. Once you've practiced on the simulator and watched our videos, it's time to start flying in real time. Be patient and don't get discouraged if you crash a few times. Everyone crashes when they're first starting out.
Join the FPV Community. You can connect with other pilots, learn from their experience, and get help when you need it.
Practice, practice, practice! The more you fly, the better you'll get. So get out there and fly as much as you can.
Don't forget to have fun! Flying FPV drones is an amazing hobby that can be enjoyed by people of all ages. So relax, have fun, and enjoy the ride!
We hope these tips help you get started in the world of FPV. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to contact us.
